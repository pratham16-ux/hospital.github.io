Our story

Built by two doctors who were tired of hospitals that felt like airports

Dr. Lakshmi Srinivasan and Dr. Mohan Bhat opened Stackly Clinic in 2008 with 40 beds, six doctors and a promise: no patient leaves confused about their own treatment. That promise scaled — the confusion didn't. Today we run 300 beds, 12 centres of excellence and a Level-III NICU, and every consultant still writes discharge notes a family can read.

  • Doctor-led, not investor-ledClinical decisions sit with the medical board — always have.
  • Teaching & research cultureDNB programmes in medicine, pediatrics and anesthesia since 2016.
  • Community firstFree rural health camps in Hoskote and Devanahalli, every quarter.

What drives us

Mission, vision and the values on our badges

Our mission: world-class, transparent healthcare within reach of every family in Bengaluru. Our vision: to be the hospital people recommend to the people they love.

Compassion

We treat the person, not just the pathology. Fear is a symptom too, and we take it seriously.

Integrity

No unnecessary tests, no surprise line items. Estimates before admission, itemised bills after.

Innovation

From robotic surgery to WhatsApp reports — we adopt technology when it helps patients, not press releases.

Togetherness

Nurses, technicians, housekeeping, consultants — one team, one patient chart, one standard of care.

Milestones

The journey so far

2008

Stackly Clinic opens

40 beds, 6 doctors and a single operation theatre on 100 Feet Road, Indiranagar.

2012

First cath lab installed

Round-the-clock primary angioplasty comes to east Bengaluru's neighbourhoods.

2014

NABH accreditation

Full hospital accreditation, renewed in every cycle since — plus NABL for our labs.

2017

New tower, 300 beds

Eight floors added: Level-III NICU, 42-bed ICU, modular OTs and a rooftop helipad-ready deck.

2021

Oncology centre of excellence

Day-care chemotherapy, weekly tumour boards and dedicated onco-counselling launched.

2024

Robotic surgery programme

Robotic-assisted joint replacement and laparoscopic urology go live, with 800+ procedures completed.
